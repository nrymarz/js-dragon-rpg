Welcome to Dragon-RPG

Instructions

  • Move around with W A S D
  • Fight dragons and win to gain XP.
  • Open your inventory with 'i' or 'esc' to see your stats and potions.
  • Move to the edge of the screen to find more enemies.
  • Gain enough levels to slay the Boss Dragon.

  • Inventory:
  • Use W and S keysii to toggle between health and mana potions.
  • If you have at least 1 of the selected potion, press "Enter" to use it.

  • Battle:
  • Select your abilites using W and S keys.
  • Use abilites with the "Enter" key
  • Spells use mana. If you don't have enough for the selected spell, you cannot use it.

  • Tips:
  • If you die or need more HP, travel around to find health and mana potions.
  • Your HP and Mana reset to the max when you level up
  • Small dragons give 20xp, Medium 40xp, Large 80xp and Very Large 250xp
